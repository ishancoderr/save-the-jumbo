Why Jumbo?

This challenge addresses a pressing global need to track change in biological diversity, which is threatened by human-driven environmental change. Use space agency data to develop innovative ways to detect biological diversity on Earth, track and predict changes over time and communicate that information to scientists and society.

SCANNING FOR LIFEFORMS

The Research

  • Intro

#Elephants #Human #Conflict

Human-elephant conflict is one of the biggest environmental and socio-economic crises of rural Sri Lanka.
Here , a low cost method was developed to find the areas frequented by elephants to reduce human elephant conflicts.

Methodology

satellite Image Analyzation

    .
  • Objectives

    • To make a risk map regarding elephant-human conflict of the relevant area.
    • To raise human safety.

  • Further Developments

    • Develop this project in many languages for the users to understand easily.
    • Develop a mobile application to view the risk area.
    • Use this project for other species of animals other than elephants.
    • Develop the project to be updated real time.
    • Use more layers to increase the accuracy(neural network).
